The Best Movies According to Nonboy

My top 3 Movies of all-time

THE TWILIGHT SAMURAI

The film is a beautifully lyrical portrayal of the life of a clumsy yet single-minded samurai.

LAPUTA: Castle in the Sky

One of my favorite animated films, it is a magnificent adventure set in the vast sky, filled with laughter, tears, and thrills, stirring the hearts of boys and girls alike.

The Shawshank Redemption

I am simply amazed and moved by the indomitable spirit. Morgan Freeman's presence is also remarkable
